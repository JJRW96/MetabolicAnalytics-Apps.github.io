4. Export Profile
ⓘ You can export two types of profiles:

- Key Efforts Profile: Exports only the identified key efforts (stars on the plot). Ideal for metabolic modeling.
- Full Profile: Exports the entire composite power curve.

To improve the Key Efforts export, you can manually remove points in the 'Key Efforts' tab or use the 'Clean Up' button to automatically remove redundant points. This ensures a balanced distribution of efforts for better model fitting.

Note: The 1s and 20s efforts are always protected and will not be removed by the clean-up process.
